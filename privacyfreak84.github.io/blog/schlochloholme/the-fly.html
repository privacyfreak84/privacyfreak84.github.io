The Fly


15th July, 2023



One day, Schlochloholme met a person looking at the sky. The person said "I wish to be a fly."


For Schlochloholme, such a wish was not odd. The wish was granted. The person was turned into a fly.


This fly went into a nearby house, where it sat on some food, contaminating it. It was happy.


At that moment, somebody came with a fly swatter and killed it flat
