Why is loserism?-

It's a good question. One that philospohers have been trying to answer since the dawn of time. I can assure you that they will never find an answer.


Why?

Because no answer exists.


Loserism happens. You can't be a conscious follower of it. If you're a follower, you will never know. It is likely that everyone is a follower, or that no one is. No one will ever know.
